## 📖 Overview

**Sangini** is the conversational AI companion developed for **Femigo**, a women's safety and empowerment platform.

Designed using **Botpress Studio**, Sangini provides natural, context-aware conversations while supporting users with emotional well-being, safety awareness, and guidance. Rather than functioning as a simple rule-based chatbot, Sangini is built as an AI-powered virtual companion that delivers personalized interactions through conversational workflows and intelligent response generation.

As part of the Femigo ecosystem, Sangini complements the application's safety features by providing users with immediate assistance, trustworthy information, and supportive conversations whenever needed.

## ⚙️ How Sangini Works

1. The user initiates a conversation through Femigo.
2. Messages are processed by Botpress Studio.
3. Botpress evaluates the conversation context and configured workflows.
4. The AI generates an appropriate response.
5. Sangini delivers empathetic guidance or relevant assistance.
6. The conversation continues naturally while maintaining context.
